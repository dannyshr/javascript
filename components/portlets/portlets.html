Control Center
Using E-OPS Control Center, you can manage your broadband network efficiently and easily.
A rich GUI client provides all variety of operations on sites and NEs, inventory, configuration, health monitoring and more.

Fault Management
Use E-OPS Fault Manager for real time monitoring of the network and the subscriber services.
Fault Manager alerts you with alarms and events analysis and historical information of occurrences.
With Fault Manager you can also set your preferences for severities, alarms descriptions and more.

Read more on Fault Manager
Octopus
E-OPS Octopus is the application to manage mass operation tasks on group of resources such as NE, cards and port.
With Fault Manager you can create and schedule various configuration tasks for a selected group of resources.
Configuration tasks can be tracked for results analysis.

Read more on Octopus

Hi-Care
E-OPS Hi-Care provide full and comprehensive look on customer configuration, real time status, recovery tools, diagnostics and more.
The Hi-Care all-in-one approach helps to accelerate the time spent on troubleshooting and ease the deployment of new services.

Reports
E-OPS Report Generator allows you to generate reports from various types, inventory, audit trail and alarms. Reports can be filtered per specific network segment or subject and can also be exported to CSV, HTML or XML files.
Report Generator is powered by Oracle.
